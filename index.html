Event Details

Location

Join us in breaking barriers and fostering innovation at our remote hackathon! Regardless of where you are, everyone is welcome to contribute their unique perspectives and skills. Let's come together as a global community to collaborate, learn, and create meaningful projects that make a difference.

Event Timing

Mark your calendars! Our exciting hackathon kicks off on February 15th, 2024, at 9:00 PM PKT, and runs until February 18th, 2024. Get ready to dive into a whirlwind of innovation and collaboration as we embark on this exciting journey together.

Team Formation and Registration

Create teams of 1 to 4 members. Designate one member to register all team members via our Google Forms link. Need teammates? Visit our Discord server to connect with others. Watch for further instructions via email after registration.

Judging Criteria

During the hackathon, your project will undergo thorough evaluation based on several key criteria. Judges will assess the effectiveness of your technology application, the clarity and impact of your presentation, the potential business value of your solution, and the degree of originality it exhibits. Aim to excel in each area to maximize your project's success and stand out among competitors. Good luck!"
